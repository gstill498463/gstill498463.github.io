PRIVACY POLICY FOR SIMPLE PLANNER Last Updated: June 15, 2024 Simple Planner ("we", "our", or "us") provides the Simple Planner mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app and the choices you have associated with that data. 1. INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE Local Data Storage All planner entries, notes, and tasks created within the app are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers, and we do not have access to your personal notes. Calendar Data If you grant the app permission to access your calendar, we use this access solely to: •Create all-day events to remind you of unfinished tasks. •Update or remove those specific reminders when tasks are completed. We do not collect, store, or transmit any other personal appointments, meetings, or contact information from your calendar. Device Information Like most mobile applications, this app may collect certain information automatically, including the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device unique ID, and your mobile operating system. 2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include: •Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt-out of interest-based ads in your device settings. •Google Play Billing: We use Google Play Billing for in-app purchases (Pro version). All financial transactions are processed securely by Google. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your credit card or bank account information.
3. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete specific notes within the app at any time. Uninstalling the Simple Planner app will remove all locally stored data associated with the app. We do not maintain any backup of your data on external servers.
